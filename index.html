About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the SAFE Lab, working on cybersecurity for Industrial Control Systems (ICS), with a strong focus on PLC memory forensics, firmware analysis, and automated vulnerability discovery.

Research Interests

  • PLC memory acquisition and forensic analysis
  • ICS protocol reverse engineering and packet profiling
  • Firmware vulnerability research
  • Machine learning for ICS anomaly detection
